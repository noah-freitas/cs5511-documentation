Final Project

In this class, we covered lots of amazing ways to use web technologies to present information in new and interactive ways. Create a webpage that discusses and demonstrates at least five Javascript techniques that you learned in this class. In your demonstration, use these techniques in new ways than you did in previous projects. This is your chance to show off what you learned to the class, to your friends and fo me. Strive to learn something new in this project, and to ask for help from me or other students, or your own discovery, to acheive your goal. Send me a brief note (by December 10) and your final link (by December 19).
